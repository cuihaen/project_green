Brand story -Never stop listening

Can a sound last 60 years? for six decades, the legacy of MARSHALL has grown - chord to chord, person to person, generation to generation.

the power to create change

60 YEARS OF LOUD

A sound that started when Jim Marshall took the time to listen to the youth in his shop and together produced a new sound, the sound of their generation. One that didn’t stop there. One that every tim e heard, inspired thenext. Till it was more than just a sound, more than just a name, it was the power to unite.

Today, you can still hear it as loud as it began. On stage, in homes and in the streets. An unwavering force founded on one simple act, listening. So the question isn’t: Can a sound last 60 years? The real question is:
